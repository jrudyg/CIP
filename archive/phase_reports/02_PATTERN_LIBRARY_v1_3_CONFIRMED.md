## PART 2: CORE PATTERNS

### 2.1 LIMITATION OF LIABILITY

#### ‚úÖ 2.1.1: Mutual Cap Pattern
**Status:** CONFIRMED  
**Revision:** Cap liability at 12-24 months fees paid OR multiplier of contract value  
**Success Rate:** 75% balanced, 85% strong leverage  
**Notes:** Multiplier approach also confirmed as valid alternative

---

#### ‚úÖ 2.1.2: Carve-Out Protection
**Status:** CONFIRMED  
**Revision:** Limit carve-outs to ONLY:
- (i) breach of confidentiality
- (ii) indemnification obligations
- (iii) gross negligence or willful misconduct

**AVOID:** "Fraud" and "violation of law" carve-outs (too broad, weaponizable)  
**Success Rate:** 60% weak leverage

---

#### ‚úÖ 2.1.3: Super Cap for IP Claims
**Status:** CONFIRMED  
**Revision:** "Notwithstanding the foregoing, liability for IP indemnification shall not exceed three (3) times the annual fees"  
**Success Rate:** 35%  
**Notes:** 3x is negotiation starting point; adjust based on deal size, IP risk, insurance limits

---

### 2.3 PAYMENT TERMS

#### ‚úÖ 2.3.1: Net 30 Standard
**Status:** CONFIRMED (Modified)  
**Principles:**
- Decrease payment terms with customers (get paid faster)
- Increase payment terms with vendors (pay later)
- Allow for milestone structures
- Allow for customer flowdown alignment

**Success Rate:** 80% strong leverage

---

#### ‚úÖ 2.3.2: Milestone Payments
**Status:** CONFIRMED  
**Revision:** Structured milestones (e.g., 30/40/30)  
**Notes:** Define milestone structure in SOW, NOT in MSA (keeps MSA flexible)  
**Success Rate:** 65% balanced leverage

---

#### ‚úÖ 2.3.3: Collaborative Dispute Resolution
**Status:** CONFIRMED (Revised from original "Dispute Rights")  
**Revision:**

> "In the event of a bona fide dispute regarding any invoice amount, the disputing party shall: (i) notify the other party in writing within fifteen (15) days of invoice receipt, identifying the specific disputed items and basis for dispute; (ii) continue payment of all undisputed amounts per original terms; (iii) engage in good faith discussions between project managers within ten (10) business days of notice. If unresolved, the dispute shall escalate to executive leadership of both parties for resolution within fifteen (15) business days. If still unresolved, parties agree to non-binding mediation before pursuing other remedies. Neither party shall suspend performance during the dispute resolution process except for material breach."

**Key Elements:**
- Written notice with specifics
- Undisputed amounts still flow
- Structured escalation (PM ‚Üí Executive ‚Üí Mediation)
- No performance suspension
