## Core Clause Clusters (Interdependency-Aware)

### Liability Protection [CLUSTER]
**When one changes, all must align:**

```
MUTUAL LIABILITY CAPS:
"NEITHER PARTY SHALL BE LIABLE TO THE OTHER FOR ANY SPECIAL, INDIRECT OR CONSEQUENTIAL DAMAGES, WHETHER ARISING FROM BREACH OF CONTRACT, TORT, NEGLIGENCE, MISREPRESENTATION, STRICT LIABILITY OR OTHERWISE, INCLUDING FOR LOST PROFITS, IMPAIRMENT OF GOODS, WORK STOPPAGE OR OTHERWISE, EXCEPT FOR GROSS NEGLIGENCE, WILLFUL MISCONDUCT, OR INDEMNIFICATION OBLIGATIONS. EACH PARTY'S MAXIMUM LIABILITY FOR DIRECT DAMAGES SHALL NOT EXCEED THE TOTAL PURCHASE PRICE PAID UNDER THIS AGREEMENT."

MUTUAL INDEMNIFICATION (ALIGNED): 
"Each party indemnifies other for claims from: (i) gross negligence/willful misconduct (ii) third-party IP infringement by their technology (iii) confidentiality breach. If claim brought against [VENDOR] based on Buyer's specifications or combination use, Buyer shall defend and indemnify [VENDOR]. If claim brought against Buyer based on [VENDOR]'s standard products used as intended, [VENDOR] shall defend and indemnify Buyer. All indemnification subject to liability limitations above."

INSURANCE REQUIREMENTS (MATCHED):
"Each party maintains: Commercial general liability $[contract value], Professional liability $1M, Cyber liability $500K. Certificates provided upon request."

Dependency Rule: Caps, carve-outs, and coverage must align across all three
Auto-Update Integration: Success patterns from 3+ sessions automatically added to alternatives
```
