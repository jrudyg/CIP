## **NDA-SPECIFIC PATTERNS (New Section)**

### **Attorney Fees Elimination (Medium to High Success)**
**Original Problem:** One-sided attorney fees favor party with more resources
**Position/Leverage:** Vendor/Balanced or Weak
**Revision:** ~~In the event of any legal action to enforce the provisions of this Agreement, the prevailing Party, as determined by a court of competent jurisdiction in a final, non-appealable decision, shall be entitled, in addition to any other relief granted, to recover its reasonable and documented costs and expenses in connection with such action, including reasonable attorneys' fees.~~ `In the event of any legal action to enforce the provisions of this Agreement, each Party shall bear its own costs and expenses in connection with such action, including attorneys' fees.`
**Success Rate:** 60% vendor seeking customer, 75% balanced leverage
**Business Context:** Eliminates disproportionate cost risk for vendor while maintaining enforceability

### **Mutual Affiliate Assignment Rights (High Success)**
**Original Problem:** Asymmetric assignment rights favor one party
**Position/Leverage:** Any/Balanced or Weak
**Revision:** ~~Notwithstanding the foregoing or anything to the contrary in this Agreement, [COMPANY_1] may assign or transfer this Agreement without the consent of the Company to any affiliate of [COMPANY_1].~~ `Notwithstanding the foregoing or anything to the contrary in this Agreement, either Party may assign or transfer this Agreement without the consent of the other Party to any affiliate of such Party.`
**Success Rate:** 75% balanced leverage, 85% vendor seeking customer
**Business Context:** Standard commercial practice, creates mutual flexibility

### **Jury Waiver Strategic Deletion (Strategic Positioning)**
**Original Problem:** Jury waiver may favor party with more resources
**Position/Leverage:** Vendor/Balanced (Vendor seeking customer)
**Revision:** ~~EACH PARTY HERETO IRREVOCABLY WAIVES ITS RIGHTS TO TRIAL BY JURY IN ANY LEGAL PROCEEDING ARISING OUT OF OR RELATED TO THIS AGREEMENT.~~ `[DELETE]`
**Success Rate:** 40% acceptance but high strategic value
**Strategic Note:** Complete deletion demonstrates reasonable pushback without harming relationship; shows standards while remaining collaborative
**Business Context:** Strategic positioning for vendors - builds respect through thoughtful engagement

### **Confidentiality Survival Period Limitation (High Success)**
**Original Problem:** Unlimited survival creates indefinite obligations
**Position/Leverage:** Any/Any
**Revision:** ~~shall survive after any termination of this Agreement~~ `shall continue for a period of three (3) years after any termination of this Agreement`
**Success Rate:** 85% any leverage, 90% with business justification
**Business Context:** Reasonable time limit while maintaining adequate protection

### **Grammar/Formatting Corrections (Very High Success)**
**Original Problem:** Typographical errors undermine document quality
**Position/Leverage:** Any/Any
**Revision:** ~~perspective~~ `prospective` (example)
**Success Rate:** 99% acceptance rate
**Strategic Note:** Easy wins that build collaborative tone and demonstrate attention to detail
**Business Context:** Creates goodwill without substantive negotiation

## LIMITATION OF LIABILITY

### Mutual Cap Pattern (High Success)
**Original Problem:** Unlimited liability exposure
**Position/Leverage:** Any/Balanced or Strong
**Revision:** ~~unlimited~~ `shall not exceed the total fees paid under this Agreement in the twelve (12) months preceding the claim`
**Success Rate:** 75% balanced, 85% strong leverage

### Carve-Out Protection (Medium Success)
**Original Problem:** Too many carve-outs to cap
**Position/Leverage:** Vendor/Weak
**Revision:** `The limitations in this section shall not apply to: (i) breach of confidentiality; (ii) indemnification obligations; or (iii) gross negligence or willful misconduct`
**Success Rate:** 60% weak leverage
**Note:** Avoid "fraud" and "violation of law" carve-outs

### Super Cap for IP Claims (Protective)
**When:** Must accept unlimited IP indemnity
**Add:** `Notwithstanding the foregoing, liability for IP indemnification shall not exceed three (3) times the annual fees`
**Success Rate:** 35% but provides some protection

## **SUCCESS RATE CALIBRATION (Session-Enhanced)**

**Factors That Increase Success Rates:**
- Mutual provisions vs. one-sided requests
- Standard commercial practice justification
- Business rationale clearly articulated
- Collaborative tone through easy wins first
- Strategic positioning appropriately balanced

**Factors That Decrease Success Rates:**
- Aggressive or demanding tone
- Requests without business justification
- Changes that appear to favor only requesting party
- Complex modifications when simple alternatives exist
- Negotiating everything rather than prioritizing critical issues

- Vendor seeking customer: +10% for relationship-preserving changes, -15% for aggressive positions
- Balanced risk tolerance: +5% for reasonable modifications, -10% for extreme positions

- Domestic operations: -20% for international compliance complexities
