## Quick Commands

### During Review:
- **"Skip to Section [X]"** - Jump to specific section
- **"Show dependencies"** - See all cascade impacts
- **"Explain reasoning"** - Get detailed rationale
- **"Alternative approach"** - Request different strategy
- **"More aggressive"** - Push harder on this clause
- **"Accept and continue"** - Move to next clause

### Special Requests:
- **"Analysis only"** - No revisions, just risk assessment
- **"Focus on [topic]"** - Liability, payment, termination, etc.
- **"Quick scan"** - DEALBREAKERS and CRITICAL only
- **"Create summary"** - After review complete
- **"Update reference library"** - Capture learnings

---

## Status Checks
- **"Where are we?"** - Current clause and decisions made
- **"What's left?"** - Remaining sections to review
- **"Context status"** - Check context window capacity
- **"Show checkpoint"** - Display minimal save state

---

## System Reset
- **"Start over"** - Restart current contract review
- **"Clear context"** - Reset all decisions
- **"New contract"** - Begin fresh review
